Why should you play gta ukraine? Why should you play в ukraine gta?

01

UKRAINIAN MAP IN THE GAME!

We have been working on the card for almost a year. Having entered in the game you will immediately understand that you are in Ukraine. The following cities are available in the game: Kharkiv, Dnipro, Gostomel, Poltava, Chernobyl and Kyiv is being developed. You will hear the whole atmosphere Ukraine, from the first seconds you will see ATB, Nova Poshta and so on!

02

REAL JOBS AND PROFESSIONS

You can become anyone, work as a truck driver for ATB or Nova Poshta . You can cut down bandits while working in the National Police or go to serve in the Armed Forces. You will have a huge choice of both beginner robots and serious professions such as: State Emergency Service, SBU.

03

Domestic and imported cars

We have added more than 400 car models. In the game you will find any car from domestic Ukrainian cars: ZAZ, KrAZ, LAZ to high-quality European, Japanese and American cars. After buying a car, you will also have something to do, because any car in the game can be tuned, you can buy new wheels, tune it and even change the characteristics of the engine.

04

Atmosphere and benefit

From the first seconds of the game, you will feel the true spirit of Ukraine . 95% of the audience speak their native language and adhere to a high level of role-playing. And thanks to our players, every month we send 50% of the donation from open cases and have already sent over UAH 300,000 to help the Armed Forces.
